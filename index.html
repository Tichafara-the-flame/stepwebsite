About Us

Europejska Fundacja Rozwoju Człowieka (EuroFRC) is a non-profit Organization based in Łódź, Poland. We provide European and global opportunities for youth and citizens based on non-formal education, cross-sectoral partnerships and cultural programmes. Our principle is anchored on the motto “humans helping humans”.

Business Models

SPLASH OF YOUR EMOTIONS, COFFEE and ECOLOGY

"Splash" is a utensils rental system in a modern city.
It's convenient, affordable and made with respect for the environm00ent.
It is partnering with cities to make streets cleaner, reducing the use of plastic in the city system by 40-50%, and making the environment accessible to everyone.

BEER AND NUTS

Good product, quality product based on customer feedback. Also, we constantly improve our products and we try to bring improvements to fulfill customers' desires.
A quality product means for us to use the best cereals and hops from biosources.
We want to build the whole business around the social and environmental aspects of development.
