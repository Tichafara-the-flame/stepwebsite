Europejska Fundacja Rozwoju Człowieka

About Us

Europejska Fundacja Rozwoju Człowieka (EuroFRC) is a non-profit Organization based in Łódź, Poland. We provide European and global opportunities for youth and citizens based on non-formal education, cross-sectoral partnerships and cultural programmes. Our principle is anchored on the motto “humans helping humans”.

Our Foundation aims at boosting the competences of young individuals at local, regional and European level by a tailor-made plan for personal and professional development and growth.

All Foundation's activities run on the basis of a regular agenda, implemented by our youth workers and project managers. We design European mobility opportunities and holistic workshops; as well we do operate as researchers in the educational sector in collaboration with local and regional stakeholders.
